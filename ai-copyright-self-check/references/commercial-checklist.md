# Commercial Checklist

Use this when the work is for a brand, client, sponsorship, paid course, product link, merchandise, marketplace, ad, or monetized account.

## Commercial Use Escalates Risk

Treat these as commercial even if payment is indirect:
- Sponsored posts and soft ads.
- Product links, affiliate links, course funnels, lead magnets.
- Client samples, pitch decks, ad mockups, brand films.
- Paid communities, paid tutorials, downloadable prompt packs.
- Merchandise, stickers, posters, print-on-demand, NFTs or token-gated goods.
- Account growth work for a company/organization.

## Pre-Delivery Checklist

Ask for or recommend preserving:
- Client asset warranty: the client confirms they own or licensed supplied logos, footage, product photos, music, and brand assets.
- Music/BGM license: exact track, license URL/file, territory, duration, media, paid-ad permission.
- Stock asset license: images, video, fonts, templates, mockups.
- Voice/likeness release: real person, actor, employee, influencer, or cloned voice consent.
- AI generation record: model, prompts, dates, reference assets, edit history.
- Final usage scope: platform, duration, territory, paid media, organic only, derivative reuse.
- Approval trail: client signoff and requested revisions.

## Contract Language To Suggest

Do not draft full legal contracts unless asked. For practical creator workflow, suggest short written confirmations:

- "Client confirms all supplied assets are owned or properly licensed for this use."
- "BGM/voice/image assets are cleared for commercial social media use on [platforms]."
- "This deliverable uses AI-generated material; client approves the final output and usage scope."
- "Any reuse outside the agreed scope requires a new rights check."

## Final Recommendation Levels

- `Ready to deliver`: licenses and scope match.
- `Deliver after fixes`: replace or document specific assets.
- `Hold delivery`: missing music/person/IP authorization in a paid context.
- `Legal review`: high-value campaign, famous IP/person, or unclear platform/license terms.
